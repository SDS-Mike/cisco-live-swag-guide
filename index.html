6 Pro tips

  1. Install the Cisco Events App first. It's the floor map, the booth numbers, the NVIDIA backpack entry, and the Cisco Live Challenge — nothing else works without it.
  2. Pre-book demos before you fly. BlueCat (Switch 2), Wyebot (Quest), ElastiFlow (Quest) all gate entry on a booked demo — qualify before you land.
  3. Badge scan is the price. Almost every giveaway is a lead-capture scan — accept the follow-up emails.
  4. Hit the time-gated prizes on the clock. The Mac Mini, the daily LEGO, and most raffles are "must be present."
  5. Go early in the week for apparel. Popular tees (NetBox Hero) and L/XL sizes run out by mid-week.
  6. Ask at every booth. Most Bronze/Copper booths run quiet scan-to-win contests they never publish.
  7. Bring a packable tote. You may be hauling a Switch, a LEGO set, and shirts home.
