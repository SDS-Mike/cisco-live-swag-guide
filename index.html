// 06_Pro_Tips

  1. Install the Cisco Events App first. Floor map, booth numbers, the NVIDIA backpack entry, the Cisco Live Challenge — nothing else works without it.
  2. Pre-book demos before you fly. BlueCat (Switch 2), Variphy (Ray-Ban Meta), Wyebot (Quest), ElastiFlow (Quest) gate entry on a booked demo/scan — qualify before you land.
  3. Badge scan is the price. Almost every giveaway is a lead-capture scan — accept the follow-up emails.
  4. Hit the timed drops on the clock. The Mac Mini, the daily LEGO, and most raffles are "must be present."
  5. Go early for apparel. Popular tees (NetBox Hero) and L/XL sizes run out by mid-week.
  6. Ask at every booth. Most Bronze/Copper booths run quiet scan-to-win contests they never publish.
  7. Bring a packable tote. You may be hauling a Switch, glasses, a LEGO set, and shirts home.
